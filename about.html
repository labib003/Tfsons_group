Tofazzal Hossain
Founder & CEO of TFsons Group

After cutting the tree, the remaining roots are picked up and sold in the market.

Gradually a capital of about 800 BDT was created and with that capital Tofazzal Hossain dared to buy a whole grass.

This is how Tofazzal Hossain's business journey started.It was 1988.

One to two, two to three, so his business scope continues to grow day by day.

Inside, he has suffered business losses many times.

But he never looked back or let his morale break.

The main principle of his business was honesty and with this honesty he was start growing his business day by day.

In the year 1998 Tofazzal Hossain started his business also in furniture sector.

His business was known day by day for his honesty and good manners and love for all his employees.

In 2010 Mr. Tofazzal Hossain decided to start business in the agro farm sector with the aim of expanding his business.And started a poultry farm business at the same time.

His Poultry Farm has suffered huge losses in just a few years of its business due to a terrible virus.

But he did not stop. He started poultry farming business from the beginning.


The number of products in each of his sectors is increasing day by day.And the number of species in the agro farming sector is increasing always.

Now his sons are getting involved in the business.

His sons are exploring new business fields with the aim of further expanding the "TFsons Group" as well as the father through honesty, trustworthiness and good behavior.
